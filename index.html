About

A Life Built Around Vedic Wisdom

Prashant Trivedi founded LotusOcean to bring Vedic wisdom into dialogue with modern life. The work spans meditation, music, literature, and film, unified by a single aim: helping people live with greater presence, balance, and connection to nature.

Outside LotusOcean, Trivedi is known for his contribution to Vedic Astrology (Jyotish). His writing on the Nakshatras is credited with reviving interest in the 27 lunar constellations within contemporary Jyotish practice.

At the center of LotusOcean is a meditation practice taught to groups worldwide, both in person and online, alongside an extensive catalogue of original music. The full catalogue and teaching schedule are hosted on the LotusOcean website.
